Who We Are

About Vrunda Natural

Vrunda Natural Pvt Ltd is a trusted supplier of premium herbal extracts, spices and dry fruits sourced from the finest natural origins across India and beyond. Founded with a passion for purity and wellness, we have built our reputation on one simple promise — delivering nature's best, exactly as it should be.

Headquartered in Dombivli, Maharashtra, our team of experts oversees every step of the journey — from careful farm-level sourcing to rigorous quality checks — ensuring every batch meets international standards before it reaches you.

What Drives Us

Mission & Vision

Two guiding principles behind every decision we make

🎯

Our Mission

To bridge the gap between nature's bounty and global wellness by supplying premium-quality herbal extracts, spices and dry fruits — with uncompromising purity, transparency and care at every step of the supply chain.

We exist to make the goodness of nature accessible to businesses and consumers worldwide, while supporting the farmers and communities that make it possible.

🌏

Our Vision

To become a globally recognised name in natural ingredient supply — known not just for the quality of our products, but for the integrity of our processes and the trust we build with every partner.

We envision a world where clean, traceable, nature-sourced ingredients are the standard — and we are committed to leading that change from India to the world.

Farm to Finish

How We Source Our Products

Every ingredient starts with a relationship — with the land and with the farmer

🌾 Direct Farm Partnerships

We work directly with verified farmers and cooperatives across India — eliminating middlemen and ensuring fair pricing at the source.

🔍 Field Inspection

Our sourcing team conducts on-site visits to verify growing conditions, harvest practices and initial quality before any procurement begins.

📋 Traceability

Every batch is fully traceable — from the farm of origin to the final shipment, giving our buyers complete confidence in what they receive.

Our Process

Manufacturing & Processing

A carefully controlled journey from raw ingredient to export-ready product

01

Raw Material Intake

Incoming materials are weighed, logged and sent for initial quality inspection before entering the facility.

02

Cleaning & Sorting

All raw materials are thoroughly cleaned, sorted and graded to remove impurities and ensure consistency.

03

Extraction / Processing

Using carefully controlled methods — drying, grinding, extraction or standardisation — to preserve active compounds.

04

Quality Testing

Every batch is lab-tested for purity, potency, microbial safety and compliance with international standards.

05

Packaging

Products are packed in food-grade, export-compliant packaging that preserves freshness and shelf life.

06

Dispatch & Export

Ready shipments are dispatched with full documentation — COA, MSDS, phytosanitary certificates and more.
