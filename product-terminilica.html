What is Terminalia Arjuna Extract?

Terminalia Arjuna Extract is obtained from the bark of Terminalia arjuna, a large deciduous tree revered in Ayurveda for over 3,000 years as a heart tonic ("Hridaya"). Its key bioactives — arjunic acid, arjunolic acid, arjunetin and tannins — provide potent cardioprotective, antioxidant and hypolipidaemic effects.

At Vrunda Natural, we supply bark powder and standardised extracts. Every batch is sourced from sustainable growing regions, tested for bioactive content, heavy metals and microbial safety, and documented with full export paperwork.
